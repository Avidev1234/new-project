Our Factory

Herbolab India Pvt. Ltd. (the manufacturing arm of Dr. Vaidya's), was established as an Ayurvedic Research Foundation in 1978 by Dr. Natoobhai Vaidya. Herbolab is housed in a 15,000 sq. ft. the stateof-the-art manufacturing facility in Silvassa (approximately 180 km from Mumbai). With access to the latest technology and best-inclass practices, the factory is run by dedicated staff, many of whom have been with the company for more than a decade.
